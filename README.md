Overview
This repository contains the completed work for the Business Sales Performance Analytics task, part of the Data Science & Analytics internship program with Future Interns.

Objective
The goal of this task was to analyze online retail sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance to provide actionable business recommendations.

Key Insights
Q4 Surge: The period from September to November accounted for 39% of the annual revenue.
Hero Products: Three products (Paper Craft Birdie, Regency Cakestand, and White Hanging Heart T-Light) generated over £411,000 in revenue.
Geographical Focus: 82% of revenue originated from the UK; however, markets like the Netherlands and EIRE show significant growth potential.
Customer Value: With an average LTV of £2,054, there is a strong opportunity to implement a loyalty program to boost repeat revenue.

Tools Used
Python: Data cleaning and preprocessing.  
Excel: KPI analysis and trend modeling. 
HTML/Chart.js: Interactive visualization dashboard.
